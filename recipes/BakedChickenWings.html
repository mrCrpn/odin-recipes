Baked Chicken Wings

Description

Chicken wings have become a staple at Super Bowl parties, and this baked recipe couldn't be easier to pull off. Try it with one of our irresistible sauces: Sriracha-Buffalo, Maple-Dijon, or Sweet-and-Sour Orange.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds chicken wings, cut in half at joints, wing tips removed
  • Coarse salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
  2. Season chicken wings with salt, then arrange in a single layer on a rimmed baking sheet.
  3. Bake until golden and cooked through, about 45 minutes.
  4. Use a thin metal spatula to transfer wings to a bowl. Toss with desired sauce.
  5. Serve.
